About Me

I am an animated filmmaker, public health researcher, and explorer of the spots where the two spheres touch. I aim to find new ways to integrate public health communication and digital media into new tools & processes that will center the needs of traditionally underserved communities in public health research/interventions.


So far, these interests have driven me to produce published public health communication research, animations about food/culture, and a web series about wheelchair travel. I am always looking for new ways to explore these intersecting interests, and right now that involves learning web development skills to be able to build more interactive projects.
